FIMP Fashion in my pocket

FIMP - Fashion In My Pocket- is an interactive app that is meant to help anyone make the most of their time when it comes to packing for trips, organizing their closet, or just picking out something to wear.

FIMP is basically a digital closet that belongs to each individual user. Everything in the closet already belongs to the owner, so it can't be confused with an online store.

With FIMP, the user could utilize the app to help them prepare for an entire outfit or an extended trip without the hassle of figuring out where the cloths are and saving time on how long it'll take.

The interactive part of FIMP comes with LED lights that can be purchased to put on hangers on each garment the user owns. That way, whenever the user selects an outfit for the next day of school or work, or perhaps the next trip, the LED light will indicate RED for an outfit that has already been selected for clothes that will be worn, YELLOW for cloths that will be packed for a trip or GREEN for cloths that are yet to be selected for anything.

Other parts of the app include a weather tab that will forecast the weather for upcoming days so the user will be able to select what to wear accordingly. FIMP will also allow users when they are out shopping. For example, when the user is out at their favorite store and is thinking about purchasing a garment but isn't sure if they have something that will match it, the user could open up the FIMP app and look through what is already in their closet to help them.

The concept for this came during the 2015 Spring Fashion / Tech Hackathon at Kent State University

Check yo self before you wreck yourself

"Check yo self before you wreck yourself" was a phone app that we developed at the 2014 Fall Fuse "Heath-care at Home" Hackathon. This was basically an aggravate, that would get information from three sources. WebMD, Mayo Clinic, and Wikipedia, and would display the symptoms and treatments for the ailment the user entered.
